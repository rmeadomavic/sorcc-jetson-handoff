Section 03 · ~30 min · OPTIONAL

Hardware: wire a Pixhawk to the Jetson.

If your platform from class was a quad, fixed-wing, rover, or boat, your Jetson talks to a Pixhawk flight controller over a serial connection. This section covers the wiring and the MAVLink link test. The Pixhawk's own ArduPilot parameters were configured in class and are not covered here.

Optional Skip this entire section if you do not have a Pixhawk, or if your Jetson is purely a compute payload (Ollama, ComfyUI, no vehicle). Nothing later in the guide depends on it.

Steps

Step 01 Wire the flight controller USB-C for bench, TELEM2 UART for flight. Three wires, one pinout, one warning about backfeeding power. ~20 min2 diagrams Step 02 Test MAVLink Install MAVProxy, connect, confirm heartbeat. Common failure modes and the fixes for each. ~10 min4 fail modes
